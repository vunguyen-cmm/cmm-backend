Calculate Your Student Aid Index

Your eligibility for need-based aid starts with colleges calculating your ability to pay for college, known as the Student Aid Index (SAI). Some colleges use only the FAFSA and its federal methodology (FM) to calculate your ability to pay, a.k.a. “FAFSA schools.” Other colleges use the CSS Profile and their institutional methodology to calculate your ability to pay, known as the Institutional Methodology Student Aid Index, a.k.a. “Profile schools.” Use the calculators below to calculate both SAIs and understand colleges’ expectations of your ability to pay.
